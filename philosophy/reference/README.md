# Philosophy & Discourse — Reference Shelf

Original, compressed summaries of real positions and the machinery of argument, organized by topic. The
agent pulls from these when session friction exposes a gap; you read them to drill a missing warrant,
then reconstruct without notes.

All files here are original educational summaries written to teach the ideas, not reproduce any text
(**CC BY-SA 4.0**). Dates and attributions follow standard scholarship.

## How to grow the shelf

Add reference files by **topic**, not difficulty. Candidates as the study expands: `epistemology.md`
(Hume, Kant), `ethics-systems.md` (Kant's categorical imperative vs. Mill's utilitarianism vs. virtue
ethics), `political-philosophy.md` (social contract: Hobbes/Locke/Rousseau), `nietzsche.md`,
`eastern-thought.md`. Keep each accurate and in your own words — summarize and cite; never paste
copyrighted text (CC BY-SA 4.0 applies to your original summaries).
